Innovation, Excellence, Simplicity in Financial Markets
 
 

FinPricing is a comprehensive cross-asset front-to-back solution that covers all investment management processes within a single platform, designed to support the entire transaction lifecycle for derivatives, securities and cash instruments, from trade capture, data service, performance monitoring and risk management, through to back office processing and reporting. Both automatic and manual interfaces are provided for each process to balance the competing demands for operational simplicity and flexibility.


FinPricing's fully integrated financial system contains a broad spectrum of cross-asset products for both buy-side and sell-side users, including but not limited to some of the world's most heavily traded securities and derivatives, such as fixed income securities and derivatives, cash and money market instruments, interest rate derivatives, equity securities and derivatives, FX spots and derivatives as well as credit derivatives. Meanwhile, we have been continuing to work on expanding our product range and footprint.


Our solution helps businesses improve operational reliability and simplicity, increase business efficiency and transparency, reduce cost and risk, maximize investment returns and profitability, and meet emerging regulatory requirements. FinPricing's financial platform - based on innovative technology, advanced analytics and broad coverage - continues to deliver products and solutions that address the complex needs of our clients in dynamical markets.
